Benefits of Installing a Cedar Roof

Cedar roofs are an increasingly popular choice for roof installments. And with good reason. There are so many benefits to installing a cedar roof and some of them include:

-It is eco-friendly. Cedar shake roofs are 100% recyclable and biodegradable and made from renewable resources. If you are looking for an environmentally sound choice, then a cedar roof may be exactly what you are looking for.
-It is a beautiful choice. If you are looking for a roof that stands out, then a cedar roof is what you need. This beautiful, natural looking roof really gives a unique feel to any house. It truly gives it a lovely appearance.
-They are extremely durable and energy efficient. Though they require a bit more maintenance than some other roofs, they will typically last longer. They also retain warm and cold air, keeping your house warmer in the winter months and cooler in the hot summer months, which will also keep your electricity bills down.
-Cedar roofing tends to be manufactured with a fire-resistant chemical, meaning that even though it is wood, it is still resistant to fires.
